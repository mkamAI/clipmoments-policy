Frequently asked questions

Why does ClipMoments need my Twitch login?
ClipMoments signs in with Twitch's official OAuth flow so it can read a channel's live chat and create clips on your behalf, the same way the official Twitch app does. We never see or store your Twitch password.

How does auto-clipping decide what to clip?
ClipMoments watches chat activity in real time and looks for spikes in message rate and "hype" keywords. When chat activity crosses a threshold, it asks Twitch to create a clip automatically.

How do I cancel my subscription?
ClipMoments Pro subscriptions are managed entirely through your Apple ID. Go to Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions on your iPhone to manage or cancel.

Is my data sent anywhere besides Twitch?
No. ClipMoments has no backend server of its own — all chat processing happens on your device, and the only network calls are to Twitch's own API.
